The problem

Most platforms reward momentum, not opportunity.

Visibility on traditional book platforms is driven by ratings, reviews, and follower counts. Books that gain early attention keep gaining more. New authors stay invisible.

📊

Algorithms favor the already-popular

Ranking systems surface books with traction, creating a loop that new authors can't enter.

👁️

Visibility is uncertain and unpredictable

Authors have no idea how many people will actually see their work, or when.

🔁

Discovery depends on social proof

Without reviews or followers, a book appears untrustworthy — before anyone has read it.
